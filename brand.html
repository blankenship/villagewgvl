Guidelines for How To Use the Village Brand

Questions? Contact joshua@joshuablankenship.com

Thank you for your interest in The Village of West Greenville, home to more than 40 unique artists, art galleries, retail shops, and restaurants! We have a few guidelines for using The Village's brand resources, so please take a moment to familiarize yourself with them. The logo is available for download below.

Our Name

The neighborhood has been known by a number of names, but as of January 2013 (and moving forward) we are officially “The Village of West Greenville.” Capitalize everything except the “of.” After identifying the neighborhood clearly, referring to us as “The Village” in subsequent mentions is acceptable.

Typography

Our primary typeface is Pompiere, a playful sans serif inspired by a handmade sign in the West Village of Manhattan. Because of its small x-height and modest weight, it will work best at large sizes, in high-impact headline situations.

Our secondary typeface is Gotham, a modern sans serif based on the unassuming utilitarian lettering of cities. We use it for subheads and body copy.
